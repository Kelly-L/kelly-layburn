Hi, I'm a digital designer and web developer that loves elegant design & clean code.

A little bit about me.

Originally from Long Island, NY, my home base is now in Charlotte, NC with my small family. My journey into technology started with a Bachelor's Degree in Visual Communications. I started as a graphic designer mostly for print. I soon became fascinated with learning to code designs for the web for it's streamlined approach to publication and the demand for work in this area. As I set out to explore coding for the web, I became immediately hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

Upon building my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. That curiosity then opened a door and earned me my first position as a web designer for a public university. After putting my career on hold for a while to raise a family, I am back in the saddle and have made a switch from design to mostly front-end development, where I can use my background in design, organization skills, and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
